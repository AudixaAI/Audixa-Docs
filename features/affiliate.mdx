The Audixa Affiliate Program allows you to earn ongoing commissions by referring new users to our platform.

<Note>
  The Affiliate Program is currently **invite-only**. You must have an invite code to join.
</Note>

## How It Works

1. **Join the Program:** Enter your invite code to access the affiliate dashboard.
2. **Share Your Link:** Copy your unique booking link and share it with your audience.
3. **Earn Commissions:** Earn **10%** commission on every payment made by users you refer.

## The Affiliate Dashboard

Once registered, you have access to a comprehensive dashboard to track your performance.

### Statistics
Track your success in real-time:
- **Total Clicks:** How many times your link was clicked.
- **Referrals:** Number of users who signed up.
- **Total Earnings:** Gross commissions earned.
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of clicks that turned into referrals.

### Balances
- **Pending Balance:** Commissions that are awaiting payout.
- **Total Paid:** Total amount you have successfully withdrawn.

## Payouts

You can request a payout once your pending balance reaches the minimum threshold.

- **Minimum Payout:** $25.00
- **Supported Methods:**
  - PayPal
  - Bank Transfer
  - Wise
  - Crypto

To request a payout, navigate to the **Payouts** tab, select your preferred method, and enter your payment details.
