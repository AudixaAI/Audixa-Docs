The Account page provides an overview of your profile, current plan, and critical account settings.

## Account Overview

This section displays your key account information:
- **Current Plan:** Your active subscription tier.
- **Profile Details:** Name, Email, and Account Creation date.
- **User ID:** Your unique internal ID (useful for support).

## Subscription Management
You can quickly check your plan features here. To upgrade or manage your billing details, click the **Manage Billing** button.

## Danger Zone

<Warning>
  **Irreversible Action:** Deleting your account is permanent.
</Warning>

If you wish to close your account, you can do so from the Danger Zone. Deleting your account will:
- Permanently remove all your personal data.
- Delete all your **Generations** and **Clone Voices**.
- Revoke all **API Keys**.
- Cancel any active **Subscriptions**.

Once deleted, this data **cannot be recovered**.

## Need Help?
If you have questions about your account or billing, you can use the **Contact Support** button to reach our team securely.
